📋 Logging a Shift
⏱
Hours Worked — Enter the total hours you worked for that shift. You can use decimals — for example, 4.5 for four and a half hours, or 0.25 increments if you want to be precise.
💰
Tips Paid Out — The card tips the store paid out to you. Cash Tips is money handed to you directly by customers. Both are required — they're added together to calculate your total earned and hourly rate.
🚗
Miles Driven — Optional. Log it if you want to track distance per shift.
☁️
Weather & Holiday — Tap to select conditions for the shift. Over time the Stats tab will show you which weather and days of the week earn the most tips.
💸 Expenses
⛽
Log job-related expenses like gas, tires, oil changes, car washes, repairs, and more. Select a category, enter the amount and date, and optionally add a description and notes.
📊
The Stats tab shows a Net Income card that subtracts your expenses from your earnings so you can see what you actually take home. A bar chart breaks down spending by category.
💵 Paychecks
📄
Log each paycheck with the amount and the date range it covers. The app will match it against shifts you've logged and calculate your base pay per hour, then combine it with tips to show your true total hourly rate in the Stats tab.
📊 Stats
🎯
Weekly Goal — Set a target earnings amount and a progress bar will appear in the History tab showing how close you are for the current week.
📅
Work weeks run Monday through Sunday. Filter by This Week, This Month, or All Time.
💾 Saving Your Data
📁
The first time you save a shift, you'll be asked where to save the file on your phone. Choose a folder (like Downloads) and it will auto-save there every time.
🔗
Each time you reopen the app, tap Relink in the blue banner to reconnect your save file. This is a one-tap step and keeps auto-saving active.
⬆
Use the Import button in the top bar to restore data from any saved backup of your file.
🚗 Drivosity Tab
🔑
Tap Clock In or Clock Out to open the Drivosity login page. Each tap is logged with a timestamp in the Recent Activity section below so you have your own record.
🍕
The Delivery Experience button opens the Domino's driver app listing so you can launch it quickly.
